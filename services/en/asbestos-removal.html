How we work

The remediation process step by step

A structured process in full compliance with Italian and European asbestos legislation.

01

Survey & assessment

Inspection and sampling to identify the presence and condition of asbestos-containing materials.

02

Work plan

Plan submitted to the local health authority (ASL) at least 30 days before work begins, as required by law.

03

Site preparation

Area demarcation and containment, decontamination unit installation and PPE distribution to all personnel.

04

Removal

Controlled removal, packaging in approved hazardous-waste bags and full site decontamination.

05

Disposal

Transport by authorised vehicles to a licensed hazardous special-waste disposal facility.

06

Final documentation

Handover of disposal certificates, transport forms and remediation completion certificate.
